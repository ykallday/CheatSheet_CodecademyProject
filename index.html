Box Model

Feature Definition Syntax
Content This area contains the actual content of an element, including text and images. It also sometimes has a background-color or background-image width and height
Padding This area is located between the content and border areas. It can be changed on the top, right, bottom and left sides. padding: 10px auto;
Border This area is located between the margin and padding areas. Their thickness and style can be changed. border: 3px solid coral;
Margin This is the outermost area in the Box Model. It borders with the margin areas of neighboring elements. It can be changed on the top, right, bottom and left sides. margin: 20px 20px;
